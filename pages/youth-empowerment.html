Boda Boda Empowerment Program

Transforming the boda boda sector through micro-financing, safety training, and sustainable electric conversion for environmental protection and increased earnings.

Low-interest loans for purchasing motorcycles, safety gear, and business equipment with flexible repayment terms.

Safety Training & Certification

Comprehensive road safety training, first aid certification, and defensive driving courses for all operators.

Electric Boda Boda Conversion

Subsidized conversion to electric motorcycles with charging stations and maintenance support for sustainability.
