High-Value Crop Programs

Diversifying from traditional crops to high-value alternatives that can multiply farmer incomes by 300-500%

Soya Beans Cultivation

Market Price: KSh 80-120/kg
Yield per Acre: 8-12 bags
Growing Season: 3-4 months
Income per Acre: KSh 60,000-100,000

Benefits:

  • Nitrogen fixation improves soil fertility
  • High protein content for nutrition
  • Ready market for animal feed and oil processing
  • Can be intercropped with maize

Avocado Farming

Market Price: KSh 15-25 each
Yield per Tree: 200-500 fruits
Trees per Acre: 100-150 trees
Annual Income: KSh 300,000-1M

Sunflower Production

Market Price: KSh 60-80/kg
Yield per Acre: 6-10 bags
Oil Content: 40-45%
Income per Acre: KSh 40,000-70,000

Macadamia Nuts

Agri-Processing Facilities

Value addition through modern processing plants that transform raw agricultural products into finished goods, creating jobs and increasing farmer incomes.

Maize Milling Plants

Modern flour mills in each sub-county with capacity to process 50 tons per day, providing ready market for maize farmers.

  • Guaranteed purchase at fair prices
  • Quality grading and premium payments
  • Reduced post-harvest losses
  • Employment for 200+ youth per plant

Oil Processing Units

Sunflower and soya bean oil extraction facilities producing cooking oil and animal feed from the by-products.

  • Complete value chain integration
  • Export quality standards
  • Branded Bungoma oil products
  • Cooperative ownership models

Dairy Processing Plants

Modern dairy facilities for milk processing, yogurt production, and cheese manufacturing with cold chain systems.

  • Daily milk collection routes
  • Quality testing and grading
  • Pasteurization and packaging
  • Regional distribution network
