"Connecting Bungoma to the World"

Revolutionary digital infrastructure that will transform how our people access information, services, and opportunities

County-Wide Fiber Optic & 5G Connectivity

Every ward, town, and village will be connected with high-speed internet infrastructure that rivals urban centers. No more digital divide between rural and urban Bungoma.

Fiber to Every Ward

Complete fiber optic coverage reaching all 45 wards with redundant connections for reliability

5G Network Deployment

Ultra-fast 5G connectivity in Bungoma Town, Webuye, Kimilili, and all major trading centers

Satellite Backup Systems

Satellite internet backup ensuring connectivity even during infrastructure maintenance

Free WiFi in Public Spaces

Markets, schools, hospitals, matatu stages, and government offices will all have free access points with educational content and government services.

24/7 Access Available
1M+ Users Expected

  • All county hospitals and health centers

E-Learning & Public Digital Hubs

Free learning zones in libraries, community centers, and refurbished containers bringing digital literacy to every corner of Bungoma.

Digital Libraries

Transform existing libraries into modern digital learning centers with computers, tablets, and high-speed internet access for research and learning.

Container Learning Hubs

Refurbished shipping containers equipped with solar power, computers, and internet serving as mobile learning centers in remote areas.

Community Centers

Upgrade community centers with digital equipment, training programs, and free internet access for adult education and skills development.

Mobile Digital Learning Vans

"Learning on Wheels" that bring digital literacy, government services, and educational content directly to rural areas that are hardest to reach.
