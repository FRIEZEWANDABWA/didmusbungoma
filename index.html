5-Point Development Agenda

Comprehensive transformation plan for Bungoma County

01

"Connecting Bungoma to the World"

Revolutionary digital infrastructure bringing high-speed internet, e-learning hubs, and smart services to every corner of our county

  • Free public learning areas in all community centers
  • Mobile digital learning vans reaching rural areas
  • Smart county services reducing corruption

02

"Unleashing the Power of Our Young People"

Comprehensive youth empowerment through digital skills, business support, and innovative financing solutions

  • Boda boda empowerment with electric conversion
  • Small business grants and mentorship
  • Export-ready youth enterprises
View Full Plan
03

"Feeding Bungoma, Feeding Kenya"

Agricultural transformation through smart farming, high-value crops, and direct market access platforms

  • Soya beans and macadamia cultivation programs
  • Agri-processing facilities for value addition
  • Digital platforms bypassing middlemen
View Full Plan
04

"Health is Wealth"

Comprehensive healthcare transformation with modern facilities, telemedicine, and community-based programs

  • Modernized health facilities with diagnostic equipment
  • County-based affordable insurance coverage
  • Mental health and wellness programs

05

"Building the Foundations of Prosperity"

Modern infrastructure development with all-weather roads, reliable electricity, and smart urban planning

  • All-weather road network to every village
  • Renewable energy projects and solar farms
  • Green Bungoma environmental program
